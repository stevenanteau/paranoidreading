Roberto Bolaño · A Season · Fall 2027

Bolaño's last novel, and the one that swallows everything. Five books in one, stitched together by a Mexican border town where the women keep turning up dead. Four scholars hunting a vanished German novelist, a professor coming apart, an American reporter in over his head, three hundred pages of forensic murder, and one soldier's entire century. It refuses to resolve, and that's the point. One book, twelve weeks, read as five separate pieces that quietly turn out to be one.

How it works. A new section drops every Sunday. Read the week's pages, then the dispatch lands on the Substack and the video on the channel. Page counts are approximate, based on the one-volume FSG / Picador edition, so go by the part, not the exact page.
