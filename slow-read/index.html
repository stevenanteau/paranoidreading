A Paranoid Reading Program

The Deep Reads

Seasons, not single books. Every run is three or four hard books read through one lens, section by section, alongside the channel and the Substack. Pick a season and read it properly.

The Seasons

Gnostic Villains on Earth

Melville, Faulkner, and McCarthy on evil that stops answering to anyone. Moby Dick, Sanctuary, and Blood Meridian, with a Gnosticism primer to sharpen the lens.
