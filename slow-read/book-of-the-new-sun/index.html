The Book of the New Sun

A Season · Winter 2027

Gene Wolfe's four-volume cycle and its coda, read straight through and read blind. Severian, a torturer with a perfect memory who can't be trusted, narrates the last days of a dying Earth. It's the hardest, richest thing in science fiction, built on buried clues, unreliable narration, and stories folded inside stories. Five books, sixteen weeks, no synopses, no wiki. Just the text.

How it works. A new section drops every Sunday. Read the week's chapters, then the dispatch lands on the Substack and the video on the channel. The current week lights up on its own.
